                 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an E-Commerce sales dataset using Excel to discover business insights, customer trends, sales performance, and profit analysis.

The analysis helps understand:

1. Top selling products
2.Monthly sales trends
3.Profit & loss patterns
4.Customer purchasing behavior
5.Region-wise performance

                     📊 Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Charts & Graphs
Data Cleaning
Dashboard Creation

   #  What is the total sales generated by the business?

# Which product category generates the highest sales?
Answer: The Technology category generates the highest sales.


# What does the high sales performance of the Technology category indicate?
Answer: It indicates strong customer demand for technology products compared to other categories.


# Do product preferences vary across different regions?
Answer: Yes, different regions have different top-selling products.


# What can be concluded from regional differences in product sales?
Answer: Customer preferences and product performance vary based on location.


# How does sales performance vary across customer segments?
Answer: Sales performance differs among customer segments.


# Which customer segment contributes the highest sales?
Answer: The Consumer segment contributes the highest sales.


# Which customer segments follow the Consumer segment in sales contribution?
Answer: Corporate and Home Office segments follow after the Consumer segment.


# What does the high contribution from the Consumer segment indicate?
Answer: It shows that individual consumers are the main source of revenue.


# Which country contributes the highest revenue?
Answer: The United States contributes the highest revenue.


# What does the United States’ revenue contribution indicate?
Answer: It indicates that the United States is the most important market for overall sales.
